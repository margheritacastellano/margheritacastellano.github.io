As part of my PhD, I develop finite-volume numerical methods for a **multiphysics system coupling two Cahn-Hilliard equations with Navier-Stokes**, modeling the evolution of two phase fields advected by an incompressible flow. Below are a few simulations produced along the way — more, longer runs to come as the methods mature.
